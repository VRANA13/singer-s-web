    Introduction and Early Career

    Indian playback singer Shreya Ghoshal is well known for her contributions to the country's music business. Shreya born on 8:00 pm on April 15th of the year 1985 . Shreya began her musical path at a young age and, by the time she was 16, was already competing in singing contests and receiving honours.

    Debut with Devdas

    debut with "Devdas" Sanjay Leela Bhansali, a director, found her and offered her a playback singingrole in his 2002 movie "Devdas" as a result. After the release of the song Bairi Piya from the film Devdas, which won her the National Film Award for Best Female Playback Singer, Shreya's career in the Indian music industry got underway.

    Awards and Recognitions

    The fact that Shreya is the only Indian vocalist to have won four National Film Awards at the age of 26 is one of her most significant accomplishments. She has also received various other honours for her services to Indian music, including four Filmfare Awards.

    Personal Life and Charitable Contributions

    Shreya is renowned for her modesty and commitment to her work. She has always made a point of expressing her passion of music and her ambition to develop as a singer. She continues to keep a modest profile and preserve her groundedness despite her enormous achievements. Shreya has been actively interested in charity efforts in addition to her singing career.
